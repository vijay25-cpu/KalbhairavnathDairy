Our Story

Founded in the heart of Maharashtra, Shree Kalbhairavanath Dairy began as a humble family-run farm with a deep respect for nature and tradition. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted name in dairy, known for our commitment to quality and community.

Our Mission

To deliver fresh, nutritious, and ethically sourced dairy products to every home, while supporting sustainable farming and empowering local farmers.
